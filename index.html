Discrete Fourier Transform Visualization

The green/blue circles in the lower window represent a 2D signal over time, a signal which is determined by the values in the frequency domain, which can be adjusted by the user.

The buttons in the top window pane represent frequencies: the little 'W' value indicates it's angular velocity, and the bar above it indicates it's magnitude.

Click the orange buttons to show/hide the red hands in the lower pane.
Drag the red hands to alter the corresponding frequency.
